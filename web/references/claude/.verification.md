## Philosophy sources (G6)

### Founder/founding facts
- **Anthropic founded January 2021** in San Francisco
  - Source: [Anthropic — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anthropic), [Contrary Research](https://research.contrary.com/company/anthropic)
- **Founders (siblings + 7 OpenAI alumni co-founders):**
  - **Dario Amodei** (CEO) — former VP of Research at OpenAI
  - **Daniela Amodei** (President) — former VP of Safety and Policy at OpenAI
  - **Tom Brown** (lead author of GPT-3 paper at OpenAI)
  - **Sam McCandlish** (Chief Architect)
  - **Jared Kaplan** (Chief Science Officer)
  - **Jack Clark** (former OpenAI Policy Director)
  - **Christopher Olah** (Interpretability Research Lead)
  - **Benjamin Mann** (Head of Anthropic Labs)
  - Others
  - Source: [Dario Amodei — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei), [Daniela Amodei — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daniela_Amodei), [TIME 100 AI 2023](https://time.com/collections/time100-ai/6309047/daniela-and-dario-amodei/)
- **Founding rationale**: split from OpenAI driven by AI safety concerns. Per Dario Amodei (in [Alex Kantrowitz's Big Technology profile](https://kantrowitz.medium.com/the-making-of-anthropic-ceo-dario-amodei-449777529dd6)): the team believed *"something in addition to just scaling the models up, which is alignment or safety"* was required.
- **Constitutional AI**: Anthropic's signature alignment technique. Defined as: training the LLM to adhere to a set of explicit principles (the "constitution"). Separates *"whether an AI can do something from the more politically fraught question of whether it should."*
  - Source: Wikipedia: Anthropic
- **Claude product line**: Claude 1 (March 2023) → Claude 2 → Claude 3 (March 2024) → Claude 3.5 Sonnet → Claude 4 family (2025) → Claude 4.7 (Opus 4.7) currently. Public availability via claude.ai, Slack, AWS Bedrock, Google Cloud Vertex AI.
- **Valuation: ~$380B as of February 2026** — privately held
  - Source: Wikipedia: Anthropic (current)
- **Series E March 2025**: $61.5B valuation; Series F September 2025: $183B; current ~$380B implies subsequent rounds
