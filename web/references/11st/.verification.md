Live DOM inspection of https://www.11st.co.kr was performed with the global
Playwright (chromium, headless, `--disable-http2`), navigating to `networkidle`
and dismissing overlays before running `getComputedStyle` on body, headings,
buttons, links, search input, product/price elements, and a full-tree color
scan. The homepage chrome is intentionally grayscale (`#666666` body on
`#111111` headings over a white field); the brand red surfaces on active/focused
states and discount accents. Over 10 real samples collected; representative set
below.
