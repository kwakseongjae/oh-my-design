## Reconciliation decisions

- The product, official V.I., and corporate/brand material are distinct domains. Only values with supplied au.com selector/surface evidence are product machine tokens, except the official `#EB5505` brand token and named official type asset, which carry their own official-doc claim paths.
- `#0066AA` is a repeated observed catalog-link colour, not an inferred au brand colour.
- Tazugane Gothic is retained as officially named brand typography, but no public browser specimen is claimed and it is never used as a fallback for `noto-sans-cjk-jp`.
- The packet has exactly one safe interaction: tab selection. Default mobile action/input/card/label geometry remains useful static evidence; unsupported hover, focus, pressed, disabled, error, dialog, toast, and responsive behavior are omitted at their own field/state boundary.
- Tier 2 was attempted twice but yielded no usable record. There is no unresolved conflict: official orange matches product use, and official font guidance is recorded without being mistaken for live product use.
