The collector reports computed use backed by a loaded FontFace and three SpaceX-hosted assets: https://www.spacex.com/D-DIN-Bold.9a5ce67e997fd030.woff2, https://www.spacex.com/D-DIN-Bold.0b92a4ce88841693.woff, and https://www.spacex.com/D-DIN-Bold.f826f81771c153ff.otf.
- **Resolution:** D-DIN is the public-web UI family and D-DIN-Bold the display family. This requires—and has—computed use, loaded FontFace matching, and SpaceX-hosted source corroboration.

### Declared-only, system, and unresolved families

- **Declared-only:** Roboto Mono has a SpaceX-hosted `@font-face` source, https://www.spacex.com/RobotoMono.19b5beb679797c73.ttf, but zero visible captured uses. It remains declared-only and is excluded from typography tokens.
- **Declared-only:** swiper-icons has zero visible captured uses and is excluded.
- **Unresolved:** D-DIN-Regular appears once in computed style but lacks a matching loaded FontFace or known system mapping. It is not treated as a separate family.

### Asset and licence boundary

- The SpaceX-hosted files above are official distributed public-web assets and corroborate the loaded-face evidence; they do not themselves grant downstream reuse permission.
- D-DIN’s upstream listing identifies Datto Inc. as foundry and lists the family under the SIL Open Font License: https://fontlibrary.org/en/font/d-din. That source identifies the upstream font licence; it does not state a SpaceX grant for copying SpaceX-hosted files.
- Roboto Mono’s upstream repository licence is the SIL Open Font License 1.1: https://android.googlesource.com/platform/external/roboto-mono/%2B/refs/heads/android17-release/LICENSE. This licence fact does not convert the zero-use declaration into a SpaceX UI token.
