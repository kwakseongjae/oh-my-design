### Color clarification: 3 Notion blues

Notion's blue palette has **three distinct blues** with separate roles:
- **`#455dd3` Notion Indigo (Primary)** — the core CTA color, used on home/product/AI/etc. Primary buttons
- **`#213183` Dark Indigo (Secondary pair)** — paired with Primary on dark/colored surfaces ("Request demo" type)
- **`#2783de` Notion Sky Blue (a11y / accent)** — reserved for skip-nav, links inside docs, and accent moments — NOT a button background

Existing §4 may use `#0072de` or `#0066cc` for "blue"; correct primary is `#455dd3`. Audit needed.

## Philosophy sources (G6)

### Founder/founding facts
- Founded **March 8, 2013** in San Francisco; Notion Labs Inc.
  - Source: [Founder Story: Ivan Zhao of Notion (Frederick.ai)](https://www.frederick.ai/blog/ivan-zhao-notion)
- Founders: **Ivan Zhao (CEO, designer/photographer background)** + **Simon Last (CTO/programmer)**
  - Source: [founderoo.co — Ivan Zhao, Simon Last, Notion](https://www.founderoo.co/playbooks/ivan-zhao-simon-last-notion)
- **First two years (2013-2015)**: built Notion Beta no-code programming tool, hired 4 employees, raised ~$2M from angel investors
  - Source: founderoo.co
- **2015 crisis pivot**: parted ways with all 4 employees, returned to square one. Ivan borrowed **$150,000 from his mother** to keep going.
  - Source: [KITRUM — Ivan Zhao](https://kitrum.com/blog/the-phenomenal-journey-of-ivan-zhao-notions-founder/)
- **Moved from San Francisco to Kyoto, Japan** (less than half cost). Two-story rented house, Shoji screen between bedrooms, **18 hours/day at laptops**, no proper meals or hygiene during the rebuild
  - Source: [Sequoia Capital — Notion Spotlight](https://sequoiacap.com/article/notion-spotlight/)
- **Notion 1.0 launched 2016**, Notion 2.0 launched on **Product Hunt March 2018** (productivity-game-changer reception)
  - Source: KITRUM
- **1M users by 2019**, viral Reddit/TikTok community-template growth
  - Source: KITRUM
- **Notion AI launched 2022-2023** as the "Augmenting Human Intellect" successor product
  - Source: [Sequoia Capital — Augmenting Human Intellect](https://sequoiacap.com/article/notion-spotlight/)
