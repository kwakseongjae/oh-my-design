## Philosophy sources (G6)

### Founder/founding facts
- **Coinbase founded June 2012** in San Francisco
  - Source: [Brian Armstrong — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brian_Armstrong_(businessman))
- Founders: **Brian Armstrong (CEO)** + **Fred Ehrsam** (former Goldman Sachs FX trader)
- **Met on Reddit** — Armstrong posted on Hacker News looking for a co-founder for Y Combinator → Ehrsam responded; the HN post went viral
  - Source: [Frederick.ai — Brian Armstrong](https://www.frederick.ai/blog/brian-armstrong-coinbase)
- **Y Combinator S12 batch ($150K initial)** in 2012
- **Mission:** "to increase economic freedom in the world" (Coinbase corporate stated mission)
- **NASDAQ direct listing April 14, 2021** under ticker **COIN** — historic moment for crypto industry
  - Source: [99bitcoins — Brian Armstrong](https://99bitcoins.com/people/who-is-brian-armstrong/)
  - Briefly approached **$100B market cap** at IPO peak
- **Survived 2022 crypto winter and FTX collapse 2022-11** — emerged as the most-regulated US crypto exchange (positioning advantage)
- **Coinbase Wallet** (separate product) — self-custody non-custodial wallet, distinct from main Coinbase exchange
- **Coinbase Prime** — institutional brokerage product
- **Base** — Coinbase's L2 blockchain (launched 2023)
