## Font evidence

### Live computed, FontFaceSet, and source corroboration

- **`bmwTypeNextWeb`:** loaded/high confidence, 288 visible uses across body, button, dialog, h1–h3, listItem, and text roles. The collector matches its computed family to loaded FontFace records and 52 BMWUSA-hosted source URLs, including `BMWTypeNext-Light`, `Regular`, `Medium`, and `Bold` WOFF/WOFF2 assets. It is the only typography-family token.
- **`BMWTypeNext`:** loaded/high confidence, five visible text uses, with six BMWUSA-hosted BMWTypeNextLatin Thin/Light/Bold WOFF/WOFF2 source URLs. It is recorded as limited live use, not promoted to a second global UI family.

### Official asset and licence boundary

- The official [BMW Club CI guideline](https://www.bmwgroup-werke.com/content/dam/grpw/websites/bmwgroup-classic_com/bmw_clubs/downloads/leitlinien/en/BCIC_Guideline_no5_version2.1.1.pdf) specifies BMW TypeNext Bold and Regular for official club identifiers. It is official distributed-brand guidance in the Club domain, not direct evidence for the BMWUSA public product surface.
- The same guideline treats BMW marks and official files as permission-bound. It is not a public webfont licence. No first-party public licence permitting downstream reuse of the BMWUSA WOFF/WOFF2 files was located. The live metadata remains useful; the files are not reusable project assets.

### Declared-only and unresolved

- **Declared-only:** Arial MT, `bmw_next_icons`, and SangBleuKingdom have `@font-face` declarations but zero visible captured uses; they are excluded from UI-family tokens.
- **No substitution:** no system font is rendered or labelled as BMW Type. The collector evidence supports BMW-loaded webfont metadata, not a fallback claim.
