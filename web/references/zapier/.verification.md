### Single-system: 4px Orange + Plum-Black inverse + warm cream canvas

Zapier uses **a 3-color tightly-bounded system**:
- **`#ff4f00` Zapier Orange Primary** — high-sat construction/safety orange (distinctive vs SaaS blue/purple)
- **`#201515` Plum-Black** — warm near-black with red undertone (NOT pure `#000`; warmer than typical inks)
- **`#fffefb` warm cream canvas** — slight warm-cast off-white (NOT pure `#fff`)
- **`#381933` Deep Plum** — purple-tinted darker variant (a11y skip-nav only)
- All radius **4px** for buttons / **18px** slight-pill for header utility / **0-0-6-6** asymmetric for skip-nav
- Weight **600** for buttons / **400** for nav — never bold-bold
- **Inter or system font** — confident reading weight

## Philosophy sources (G6)

### Founder/founding facts
- **Zapier founded 2011 in Columbia, Missouri**
  - Source: [Zapier — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zapier)
- **Co-founders**: **Wade Foster** (CEO), **Bryan Helmig**, **Mike Knoop** — three University of Missouri grads
- **Origin**: prototype built during a Columbia startup weekend
- **Foster left job December 2011** → **Helmig April 2012** → **Knoop full-time May 2012** (same month as public beta)
- **YC initially rejected** (janky weekend prototype, no customers) → **accepted on second try → YC W12 batch June 2012** (relocated to Mountain View)
  - Source: [Q&A with Wade Foster — Y Combinator](https://www.ycombinator.com/blog/qa-with-wade-foster-cofounder-of-zapier/)
  - Source: [Zapier — Y Combinator company page](https://www.ycombinator.com/companies/zapier)
- **Seed $1.2M October 2012** led by **Bessemer Venture Partners + Draper Fisher Jurvetson** + angels
- **Minimal outside funding thereafter** — uncommon for SaaS at scale (bootstrap-ish discipline)
  - Source: [Side hustle to $35M ARR at Zapier with Bryan Helmig — Founders Talk #55](https://changelog.com/founderstalk/55)
- **Remote-first since founding** (no office, fully distributed) — pioneered in 2012, well before 2020 industry shift
- **2024-2025**: AI agents + MCP integration alongside classic Zaps
