## Logo Decision

- Google favicon (`https://www.google.com/s2/favicons?domain=doordash.com&sz=128`): 452 bytes (PNG 32×32) — above 450-byte floor, valid real favicon. Borderline.
- `https://cdn.simpleicons.org/doordash`: HTTP 200, `image/svg+xml` — DoorDash is in the SimpleIcons catalog. Preferred over favicon (scalable SVG, no byte ambiguity).
- **Final**: `type: simpleicons, slug: doordash` (HTTP 200, SVG confirmed)
