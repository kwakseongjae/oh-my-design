### Conflict resolution: Light Pill vs Neon Lime Primary

Linear's marketing site has TWO primary CTA appearances:
1. **Light Stone Pill** (`#e5e5e6` 9999px) — header Sign-up, current home
2. **Neon Lime 6px** (`#e4f222`) — captured by refero, used in specific hero / section CTAs (e.g. "Build now" feature blocks)

Both are real. Refero is documenting a **specific feature-block CTA pattern** that uses Lime; the header Sign-up uses the Light Stone Pill. Linear's brand color is **Indigo `#5e6ad2`** (Skip Nav, classic Linear product accent), and Lime `#e4f222` is reserved for **launch / hero moments**.

**Resolution:** Document THREE primary patterns in §4:
- Light Stone Pill (header CTA) — `#e5e5e6` 9999px
- Indigo Skip / Brand Accent — `#5e6ad2`
- Lime Launch CTA — `#e4f222` 6px (refero-captured, hero/launch moments)

### Unresolved
- **None.** All three primaries documented as discrete variants.
