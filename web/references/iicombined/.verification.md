# IICOMBINED — Verification Notes (2026-06-17)

IICOMBINED (아이아이컴바인드) is the creative house behind Gentle Monster (eyewear),
Tamburins (fragrance), and Nudake (dessert/art). The house has no thick standalone
storefront of its own (iicombined.com is a thin corporate page), so the canonical
homepage documented here is gentlemonster.com — the richest, most representative
house surface — cross-read against tamburins.com as the second brand-owned surface.
