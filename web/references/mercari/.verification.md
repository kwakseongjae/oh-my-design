## Font evidence and licensing boundary

- **Averta — verified live public-web use.** The collector reports `Averta` as `loaded`, high confidence, with 902 computed uses across body, button, card, dialog, heading, input, list-item, and text roles. It associates eight Mercari-hosted source URLs: light, regular, semibold, and bold WOFF/WOFF2 files under `https://u-web-assets.mercdn.net/assets/fonts/averta/`.
- **Averta aliases.** `averta-semibold` is loaded with 27 visible uses and `averta-regular` is loaded with three; both are treated as aliases of the verified Averta family rather than standalone typography tokens. `averta-bold` is declared with zero visible uses and remains declared-only.
- **Official product-use boundary.** No official Mercari announcement reviewed in this run says Averta is the native mobile-app or authenticated-marketplace family. The verified scope is the captured public web.
- **Publisher/license boundary.** Foundry5 lists Averta PE in its public catalogue and its EULA covers commercial webfont `@font-face` use. Neither source gives this repository’s users a Mercari font-file license, nor proves that the retail package is identical to Mercari’s CDN file. [Foundry5](https://foundryfivetype.com/) · [Foundry5 EULA](https://foundryfivetype.com/eulas/)
- **System/unresolved.** Arial appears in declarations beside some Averta aliases but has no independent loaded FontFace/source corroboration. It is not a UI-family token and must not be substituted while being labelled Averta.

## Context and narrative evidence

- [Mercari listing guide](https://www.mercari.com/us/help_center/topics/listing/guides/creating-a-listing/) describes the public US listing path as photos, description, price, then shipping. It supports marketplace context and practical public voice; it is not visual-token evidence.
- [Mercari marketplace guidelines](https://www.mercari.com/us/help_center/article/407/) addresses buyers and sellers as real people and organizes guidance around neighborliness, safety, and legality. It supports voice/principle context; it is not component evidence.
- [Mercari marketplace principles](https://pj.mercari.com/principles/marketplace-principles-and-history_EN.pdf) states the group mission and discusses a safe, trustworthy, humane marketplace and circular economy. It supports narrative/principle context; it is not a public-web token source.
- [Mercari brand directory](https://www.mercari.com/us/brand/) is a public product directory of brands, not a brand-system guide. Its capture is kept separate from corporate and marketplace claims.
