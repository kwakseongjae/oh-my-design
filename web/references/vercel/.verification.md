  - Source: [History of Vercel 2015–2020 (Medium)](https://medium.com/history-of-vercel/history-of-vercel-2015-2020-6-7-zeit-and-next-js-dc480a88e0b8)

  - Source: Wikipedia: Vercel
- **May 2024 funding:** $250M raised at **$3.25B valuation**
  - Source: [BrandHistories — Vercel](https://brandhistories.com/vercel/company-history)

  - Source: WebSearch consensus
