## Philosophy sources (G6)

### Founder/founding facts
- **Uber founded 2009** in San Francisco as **UberCab**
  - Source: [Travis Kalanick — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Travis_Kalanick)
- Founders: **Garrett Camp** (Canadian, **co-founder of StumbleUpon**) + **Travis Kalanick**
  - Source: [Garrett Camp — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Garrett_Camp)
- **Origin**: Camp frustrated with SF taxis + expensive black-car services → conceived smartphone-app for hailing luxury vehicles → discussed with Kalanick who joined as "mega advisor"
- **Ryan Graves**: launch CEO for **10 months** before being replaced by Kalanick
- **Camp + Graves transferred shares to Kalanick** on his CEO transition
- **2018 rebrand by Wolff Olins + Uber in-house team** (10-month project): **Uber Move** bespoke typeface + monochrome black-on-white wordmark + bespoke **Safety Blue** accent
  - Source: [Design Week — Uber rebrand 2018-09](https://www.designweek.co.uk/issues/17-23-september-2018/uber-rebrand-looks-to-reflect-how-the-taxi-app-is-changing/)
  - Source: [Dezeen — Uber brings back the U](https://www.dezeen.com/2018/09/14/uber-rebrand-wolff-olins-black-white-wordmark-logo-typography/)
- **NYSE IPO May 10 2019** ticker **UBER** at **$45/share, 180M shares = $8.1B raised at ~$75B valuation** — biggest IPO of 2019, top-10 ever
- **First-day close $41.57** — biggest first-day dollar loss in U.S. IPO history
  - Source: [Uber IR — IPO pricing](https://investor.uber.com/news-events/news/press-release-details/2019/Uber-Announces-Pricing-of-Initial-Public-Offering/default.aspx)
  - Source: [Bloomberg — Uber drops after $8.1B IPO 2019-05-10](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2019-05-10/uber-drops-after-raising-8-1-billion-in-biggest-ipo-of-2019)
- **Dara Khosrowshahi** (ex-Expedia CEO) succeeded Kalanick as Uber CEO **August 2017**
