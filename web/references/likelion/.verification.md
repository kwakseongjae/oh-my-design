## Font evidence

### Computed, FontFaceSet, and source corroboration

- **Pretendard Variable — unresolved marketing computed face:** 193 visible uses across homepage body, buttons, cards, headings, inputs, list items, and text. It has no matching loaded FontFaceSet record or source URL in the supplied evidence. It is not promoted to `tokens.typography.family`.
- **Pretendard — loaded documentation-chrome face:** 34 visible uses on documentation controls/inputs/text. The collector marks it loaded/high and records 22 sources, including LikeLion-hosted `https://likelion.net/fonts/Pretendard-Regular.subset.woff2`, `Pretendard-Medium.subset.woff2`, `Pretendard-SemiBold.subset.woff2`, and `Pretendard-Bold.subset.woff2`, plus upstream CDN paths. This provides computed use plus FontFaceSet/source corroboration for documentation chrome only.
- **Declared-only:** Gotham (three LikeLion font sources), HeirofLight (two sources), Noto Sans Mono (three sources), Nunito Sans, and slick (five icon-font sources) have zero visible captured use. They remain declared-only and are not specimens or UI-family tokens.

### Official font and licence boundary

- The upstream Pretendard project publishes Pretendard under SIL Open Font License 1.1: https://github.com/orioncactus/pretendard/blob/main/LICENSE
- That upstream licence supports the identity and licence context of Pretendard. It does not turn a homepage’s unresolved `Pretendard Variable` computed family into a loaded match, and it does not grant a licence for Gotham, HeirofLight, Noto Sans Mono, Nunito Sans, or slick.
- No LikeLion-authored font licence or official announcement establishing a distinct product font was found in the requested source search. No substitute family is authorized.
