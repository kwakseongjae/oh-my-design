### Founder/founding facts

  - Source: [Aidan Gomez — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aidan_Gomez), [Cohere About](https://cohere.com/about)

  - Source: [Wikipedia: Aidan Gomez](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aidan_Gomez)
