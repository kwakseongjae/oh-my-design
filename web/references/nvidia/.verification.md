### Founder/founding facts
- **NVIDIA founded April 5 1993** at a **Denny's in East San Jose** with **$40,000** initial capital
  - Source: [Nvidia — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nvidia)
  - Source: [NVIDIA blog — Huang returns to Denny's](https://blogs.nvidia.com/blog/nvidia-dennys-trillion/)
- Founders: **Jensen Huang (CEO)** + **Chris Malachowsky** + **Curtis Priem**
  - **Huang**: Taiwanese-American electrical engineer; ex-CoreWare director at LSI Logic; ex-AMD microprocessor designer; **first job: Denny's dishwasher → waiter** (cited publicly as biographical detail)
    - Source: [Jensen Huang — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jensen_Huang)
  - **Malachowsky**: ex-Sun Microsystems engineer
  - **Priem**: ex-IBM/Sun Microsystems engineer
- **Founding booth received plaque "The NVIDIA Booth — The booth that launched a trillion-dollar company"**
- **1999**: shipped what NVIDIA called the **first GPU**
- **2006**: opened parallel architecture to general computation with **CUDA**
- **2012**: GPU-trained **AlexNet** wins ImageNet — modern AI era effectively dated here
- **2022**: **H100 (Hopper)** shipped — 80GB HBM, 4th-gen Tensor Cores, FP8, Transformer Engine
  - Source: [NVIDIA H100 product page](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/data-center/h100/)
- **July 9 2025**: **first company in history to reach $4T market cap**
  - Source: [NBC News — NVIDIA $4T](https://www.nbcnews.com/business/business-news/nvidia-becomes-first-company-worth-4-trillion-what-to-know-rcna217721)
- **October 30 2025**: **first company in history to reach $5T market cap**
  - Source: [CNBC — Huang $5T NVIDIA 2025-10-30](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/10/30/how-jensen-huang-turned-nvidia-into-the-first-5-trillion-company.html)
