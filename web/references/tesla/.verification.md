### Founder/founding facts
- **Tesla Motors incorporated July 2003**
  - Source: [Tesla, Inc. — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tesla,_Inc.)
- Founders: **Martin Eberhard** + **Marc Tarpenning** (original Silicon Valley engineers)
  - Source: [Britannica — Eberhard + Tarpenning](https://www.britannica.com/money/Martin-Eberhard-and-Marc-Tarpenning)
- **Eberhard original CEO** until late 2007
- **2009 settlement**: All five — **Eberhard, Tarpenning, Ian Wright, Elon Musk, J.B. Straubel** — recognized as co-founders
- **Musk joined February 2004** — Series A $7.5M including $6.5M from Musk's PayPal-exit funds; became chairman + largest shareholder
- **Straubel joined May 2004** as 5th employee + CTO; **15 years as CTO** until **July 2019** advisory transition
  - Source: [J.B. Straubel — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/J._B._Straubel)
- **Musk became CEO October 2008**
- **NASDAQ IPO June 29 2010** — first American car company to IPO since Ford 1956 — 13.3M shares at **$17/share, raised $226M**
  - Source: [Tesla IR — IPO pricing](https://ir.tesla.com/press-release/tesla-announces-pricing-initial-public-offering)
- **Roadster production 2008-2012**; **Tesla Factory opened October 2010**; **Model S launched June 2012**
- **Cybertruck delivery event November 30 2023**, base price $60,990 (originally announced 2019 at $39,900)
  - Source: [Tesla Cybertruck — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tesla_Cybertruck)
- **Current vehicle lineup (April 2026)**: Model 3, Model Y, Semi, Cybertruck, Cybercab
- **2025**: 1.66M vehicles delivered, 46.7 GWh battery storage deployed
- **Master Plan**: Part 1 (2006) → Part 2 (2016) → **Part 3 (April 2023, 41-page paper)** → **Part IV (September 2025)** "sustainable abundance"
