### Subpixel notes

### Founder/founding facts
- **Wise founded January 2011 in London** (originally TransferWise; renamed July 2012; rebranded Wise February 2021)
  - Source: [Wise (company) — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wise_(company))
