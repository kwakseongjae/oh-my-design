### Single-system: 9999px Black/White pill at three height tiers

ElevenLabs uses **one consistent button shape** across home + pricing:
- All radii **9999px** full-pill
- Two fills only: `#000` (Primary) + `#fff` (Inverse)
- Three height tiers: **48px hero**, **40px mid-page**, **28px header**
- Font 14-16px·400 — never bold; the radius and contrast carry weight
- Padding scales with height: 0×20 / 0×16 / 0×12

This is one of the cleanest single-system documents in the corpus — pure binary B/W chrome with three discrete height tiers. Confirms the §11 "research-first / clinical-precise" positioning (no decorative color in chrome).
