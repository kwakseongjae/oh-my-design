### Single-system: 12px Kraken Purple + tiered radius scale

Kraken uses **one button geometry with a tiered radius scale**:
- All CTAs: `#7132f5` Kraken Purple Primary 12px (header 36px / page 48-52px / weight 500)
- Light Purple ghost variant: `rgba(133,91,251,0.16)` 12px — distinctive translucent fill that maintains the Purple identity
- Light Secondary `#f5f5f5` 12px for inverse on dark hero
- **Filter pills** drop to 10px (sub-CTA hierarchy) — intentional radius distinction
- **Currency/utility selectors** drop to 8px (deepest sub-tier)

Three discrete radii (12 / 10 / 8) communicate three discrete hierarchies: Action > Filter > Selector. All weight 500. Padding scales with height.

This is one of the cleanest financial-tier chrome systems in the corpus — every radius value carries semantic meaning.
