This split is intentional and parallels the karrot dual-canvas pattern: consumer = playful brand voice, corporate = serious institutional voice.

## Philosophy sources (G6)

### Founder/founding facts
- **LINE launched June 2011** in Japan by **NHN Japan** (subsidiary of Korean **Naver/NHN**) in response to **March 11 2011 Tōhoku earthquake/tsunami**
  - Source: [LINE — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Line_(software))
  - Source: [Line Corporation — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Line_Corporation)
- **Naver/NHN co-founder Lee Hae-Jin** (이해진) was in Japan when the disaster struck; tasked NHN Japan team with building messenger
- **Beta version testing within 2 months** of disaster → public launch June 2011
- **April 1 2013**: NHN Japan renamed **LINE Corporation**
- **December 2020**: LINE Corp delisted NYSE + Tokyo SE
- **March 1 2021**: SoftBank-affiliated **Z Holdings** (Yahoo! Japan operator) merger with LINE; **A Holdings** (SoftBank + Naver) holds 65.3% Z Holdings
- **October 1 2023**: LINE + Z Holdings + Yahoo Japan merge into **LY Corporation** — LINE's independent operations end
  - Source: [LY Corporation](https://www.lycorp.co.jp/en/)
- **2024**: LY Corp announced plans to **sever tech ties with Naver by year-end** following Japan's regulator scrutiny over data security
  - Source: [KED Global — LY-Naver tech tie cut 2024-06](https://www.kedglobal.com/business-politics/newsView/ked202406190005)
