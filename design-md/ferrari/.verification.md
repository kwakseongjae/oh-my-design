### Single-system: 0px Ferrari Red Primary + ALL CAPS ghost chrome

Ferrari uses a **dual-discipline chrome system**:
- **Primary CTA**: `#da291c` Ferrari Red, **0px sharp**, square padding (21×21), white text, 16px·400 ALL CAPS
- **Brand chrome (nav/footer)**: text-only ALL CAPS ghost links, no fills, no radii — the cinematic imagery carries the visual weight
- **Cookie banner exception**: 2px radius / 13.008px·600 — separate utility track for GDPR

The product discipline is **chiaroscuro** (per §11): black hero + white car-range + red CTAs at the moment of action. Not a single bonus color. The 0px sharp radius ties to the Italian-precision claim in §12.

This corrects the prior footer's "cookie banner CTAs" claim — the canonical Primary is Ferrari Red 0px, not the cookie-banner 2px white.

## Philosophy sources (G6)

### Founder/founding facts
- **Scuderia Ferrari** founded **1929** by **Enzo Ferrari** — Grand Prix racing team affiliated with Alfa Romeo (Enzo had worked there in the 1920s)
  - Source: [Enzo Ferrari — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Enzo_Ferrari)
- **Ferrari S.p.A.** founded **1947** in **Maranello** (relocated from Modena after WWII Allied bombing)
  - Source: [Ferrari — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ferrari)
- **First Ferrari road car: 125 S (1947)** — V-12 engine became the brand signature
- **First Grand Prix victory: Rome, May 1947**
- **NYSE direct listing October 21, 2015** under ticker **RACE** — IPO priced at **$52/share** (top of $48-52 range), market cap **~$9.8B**, raised ~$900M public capital, 17.18M shares = ~9% of company
  - Source: [CNBC — Ferrari market debut](https://www.cnbc.com/2015/10/21/ferrari-spikes-15-in-market-debut.html)
  - Source: [Ferrari Corporate — Listing information](https://www.ferrari.com/en-EN/corporate/listing-information)
- **Spun off from Fiat Chrysler (FCA) October 2015** as part of demerger
- Ferrari Red `#da291c` is the official brand red — used sparingly per design discipline
