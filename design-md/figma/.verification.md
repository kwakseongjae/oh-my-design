### Conflict resolution: Primary Pill radius (8px vs 50px)

**Investigation:** refero claims Figma Primary is 50px pill. Live Tier 1 inspect on `figma.com/ko-kr/` shows Primary CTAs ("지금 무료로 시작하기", "시작하기") use **8px** radius. The **50px** pill in live observations is the **Segmented Tab** for product nav (`.fig-111738k`), not Primary.

**Hypothesis:** refero's "Global Action Pill 50px" likely captured the Segmented Tab Pill OR a legacy variant. The contemporary figma.com Primary CTA is unambiguously 8px.

**Resolution:** **Live Tier 1 wins.** §4 documents:
- Primary Pill: `#000` 8px (the actual Primary CTA observed live)
- Segmented Tab Pill: 50px (separate variant for product navigation)

This explains the previous figma DESIGN.md confusion — "Black Solid (Pill)" with `Radius: circle (50%) for icon buttons` was conflating Primary, Tab Pill, and Icon Round into one entry.
