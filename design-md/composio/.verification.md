### Single-system: 0px UPPERCASE Black/White terminal

Composio uses **one consistent button system** across home + pricing:
- All radii **0px** (sharp — terminal/CLI aesthetic, no rounded chrome anywhere)
- All labels **UPPERCASE** (intentional terminal register)
- Two fills only: `#000` (default) and `#fff` (inverse on dark hero)
- Font 14px·400 abcDiatype across header/hero (12px on cards)
- Heights graduate by context: 30 (card) / 32-33 (header) / 39-40 (hero)

This is one of the strictest single-system documents — sharp 0px is the entire personality, distinguishing Composio from every rounded-pill SaaS competitor.
