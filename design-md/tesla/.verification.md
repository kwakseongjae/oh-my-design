## Font evidence

### Live computed, FontFaceSet, and source corroboration

- `Universal Sans Text` is loaded with high confidence, has 266 visible uses, and is used for badges, body, buttons, cards, dialogs, list items, tabs, text, and toggles. The collector matches visible computed use to loaded FontFace entries and Tesla-hosted regular, medium, bold, light, and thin WOFF2 sources under `digitalassets.tesla.com/tesla-design-system/raw/upload/static/fonts/universal-sans-2/web/text/`.
- `Universal Sans Display` is loaded with high confidence, has 65 visible uses, and is used for headings and display copy. The collector matches it to Tesla-hosted regular, medium, bold, light, and thin WOFF2 sources under `digitalassets.tesla.com/tesla-design-system/raw/upload/static/fonts/universal-sans-2/web/display/`.
- Resolution: the two loaded families are the only typography-family tokens, split into UI/Text and Display roles. This conclusion uses computed family, loaded FontFace status, and official asset source together.

### Official asset and licence boundary

- Official font assets: https://digitalassets.tesla.com/tesla-design-system/raw/upload/static/fonts/universal-sans-2/web/text/Universal-Sans-Text-Regular.woff2 and https://digitalassets.tesla.com/tesla-design-system/raw/upload/static/fonts/universal-sans-2/web/display/Universal-Sans-Display-Regular.woff2.
- Tesla’s public legal terms are at https://www.tesla.com/legal/terms. They are a legal/ownership boundary, not a published Universal Sans font licence. No first-party public web-font licence permitting downstream reuse was found in the requested source search. The reference therefore keeps family metadata but does not treat the captured files as reusable project assets.

### Declared-only, system, and unresolved families

- **Declared-only:** Blender TSL, CT Speed, Fira Code, Noto Sans (including Arabic, Hebrew, and Thai variants), Open Sans, and PingFang variants appear in `@font-face` declarations but have zero visible captured use. They remain declared-only.
- **Unresolved documentation face:** Times appears in the owners-manual computed styles but has no matching loaded FontFace or source URL. It is neither system-mapped nor promoted as Tesla typography.
- **Domain boundary:** no authenticated Tesla app, in-car UI, account flow, or source-backed documentation typography was supplied. Public Model 3 webfont evidence does not populate those domains.
