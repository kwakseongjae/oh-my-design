## Font evidence

### Live computed, FontFaceSet, and source corroboration

- **Inter — official product-use and live computed marketing use:** Resend’s official brand guidelines name Inter by Rasmus Andersson as its product typeface. The collector also records 599 visible uses across body, buttons, menus, tabs, toggles, headings, and text, matched to a loaded FontFace and the Resend-hosted source `https://resend.com/_next/static/media/inter_variable.p.0r27kd5h06n72.woff2?dpl=dpl_6QdhL9DCTN9ExbvTURDkdxpxJgKR`.
- **ABC Favorit — official display-use and live computed marketing use:** the official guidelines name ABC Favorit by Dinamo as the display typeface. The collector records 40 visible heading uses, matched to loaded Book and Medium Resend-hosted WOFF2 sources. Dinamo describes Favorit as a low-contrast grotesque; its commercial licence is a Dinamo EULA, not a reusable Resend asset licence.
- **Domaine Display — official editorial-use and live computed marketing use:** the official guidelines name Domaine Display by Klim Type Foundry as editorial type. The collector records four visible `h1`/`h2` uses and matches them to three loaded Resend-hosted Domaine WOFF2 sources. Resend’s 2025 rebrand article says Domaine is used for editorial headlines. No public first-party downstream reuse licence for the captured Resend files was found.

### System, declared-only, and unresolved families

- **System-computed:** Helvetica appears 20 times in the collector, including the observed `#00a3ff` primary CTA. It has no recorded loaded FontFace/source URL, so it remains a system-computed surface value rather than a Resend typography-family token.
- **System-computed:** `-apple-system` appears twice in an embedded editable field. It is not a Resend family token.
- **Declared-only:** `commitMono` has two Resend-hosted `@font-face` source URLs but zero visible captured uses. It remains declared-only; no code-panel token or specimen is made from it.
- **Boundary:** the three captured routes are marketing pages. Their loaded faces do not describe the authenticated dashboard, API output, generated emails, or documentation chrome.
