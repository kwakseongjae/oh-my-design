### Surface split: Marketing 12px / Product 8-10px

Cal.com runs:
- **cal.com/ (marketing)** — Apple-inspired cinematic monochrome, 12px radius primary, 39px height
- **i.cal.com/<user>/<event> (booking product)** — tighter 8-10px radius, 30-59px components, calendar-grid focused
- **app.cal.com (in-product dashboard)** — likely shares Booking page chrome but auth-walled

This is a typical marketing/product surface split (similar to Apple, Stripe, Vercel patterns).
