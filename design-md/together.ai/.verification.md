### Single-system: 4px Black Pill + ALL CAPS 500 + canvas-aware translucent

Together AI uses a **disciplined two-color chrome with canvas-aware translucent variants**:
- All Primary CTAs: **`#000` Black 4px / 40px** (32px header) / 16px·**500 ALL CAPS**
- **Light-canvas Outline**: `rgba(0,0,0,0.08)` 8% Black ghost + `#000` text
- **Dark-canvas Outline**: `rgba(255,255,255,0.12)` 12% White ghost + `#fff` text
- **Pricing tab sub-tier**: 3.25px radius (slightly less round than 4px Primary) + 13px·500
- All weight 500 — never bold; ALL CAPS carries the engineering register

The **3.25px tab radius** is unusual — most brands use 0px or 4px for tabs; Together's slightly-less-round 3.25px is a deliberate sub-tier indicator. ALL CAPS 500 weight + Black-only canonical = academic-engineering register.

This corrects the prior footer's claim of "Start building `#000` 3.33px / 13.3px / 33px / 13.3px·500" — the 3.33px / 13.3px / 33px values are **rendering artifacts of subpixel rounding** (likely from `rem` units at non-1.0 root font-size). Tier 1 normalized measurement shows **canonical 4px / 16px / 40px / 16px·500**. Material correction.
