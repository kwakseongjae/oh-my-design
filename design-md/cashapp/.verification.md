### Documented palette (design.cash.app/color — verbatim)
- Cash Green — CMYK 65/0/100/0, RGB 0/224/19, **HEX #00E013**, PMS 802 C / U C  → matches live `rgb(0,224,19)`
- Black — CMYK 60/40/40/100, RGB 0/0/0, **HEX #000000**, PMS Black C
- White — CMYK 0/0/0/0, RGB 255/255/255, **HEX #FFFFFF**, PMS Bright White C
- Citron — CMYK 11/0/91/0, RGB 216/255/20, **HEX #D8FF14**, PMS 388 C / 387 U
- Notes: "Cash Green should be omnipresent across all brand touchpoints and used strategically such that it stands out as the hero color." / "Citron can be used sparingly as a high-impact accent... should never be used as a full background fill." / "Black and white provide balance to Cash Green."

### Documented typography (design.cash.app/typography — verbatim)
- "Our typeface is Cash Sans, a customized version of Söhne by Klim Type Foundry. A neo-grotesque sans serif."
- "The primary feature specific to Cash Sans that distinguishes it from Söhne is rounded punctuation."
- "Regular and Medium are our default for typesetting."
- Secondary typeface section exists for Display / Novelty / Bespoke usage.
