### Two-system: Consumer hyperlocal + Corporate brand chrome

Karrot uses **two distinct chrome systems**:
- **Consumer (daangn.com)**: Karrot Orange `#ff6600` 4px sharp Primary (32px / 14px·**600**) + Cool Gray `#f3f4f5` 9999px neighborhood pills + Karrot Charcoal `#2a3038` skip-nav
- **Corporate (about.daangn.com)**: Karrot Deep Charcoal `#1a1c20` 999px full-pill (50px / 16px·400) — completely different shape and weight from consumer

This dual-canvas split is intentional: consumer surfaces use **functional, smaller, weighted** chrome (transactional UX), while corporate surfaces use **larger, lighter, pill-shaped** chrome (brand-led). The neighborhood pill at 9999px is the visual signature regardless of canvas.

The orange `#ff6600` is **only** on consumer-product surfaces — never on corporate.
