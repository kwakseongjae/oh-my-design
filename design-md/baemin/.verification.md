## Context and narrative evidence — 2026-07-12

- https://www.woowahan.com/en/company/history — official history: Baemin launched in 2010 to advance food delivery through information technology; Hanna became an integral brand symbol and widened Hangeul font diversity.
- https://www.woowahan.com/en/service/culture — official culture page: Baemin presents original goods, fonts, and music as cultural assets; the current page describes 13 freely usable fonts.
- https://www.woowahan.com/fonts — official catalog: Hanna's 1960s–70s acrylic-sign origin; Jua's hand-painted storefront character; Dohyeon's methodical acrylic-cut forms and connected strokes; Euljiro weathering concept; Kkubulim's bent-edge character.
- https://www.woowahan.com/fonts/license — official license boundary: commercial/non-commercial use and modification are allowed under the stated license; standalone sale of OTF/TTF files is prohibited and redistribution must preserve license conditions.
- https://techblog.woowahan.com/2511/ — first-party historical account: annual font making began in the early 2010s and the program connected type development to internal culture and Korean sign-lettering research.
- https://www.woowahan.com/report/detail/975?page=1 — official Baemin 2.0 product direction: brighter mint, WORK app typeface, progressive customer-centered redesign, and renewed service mission/principles.
