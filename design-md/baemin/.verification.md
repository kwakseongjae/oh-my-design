### Surface split — App vs Web

The famous **Mint `#2AC1BC` Primary** of 배달의민족 lives **inside the mobile app**. The web surfaces (baemin.com / woowahan.com) use achromatic pills (white/black/light-gray). This is intentional — web surfaces are corporate/marketing, the brand color lives in the actual app where it functions as Action color.

The app surface (Mint Primary, Hannache typography for marketing illustrations, system font for chrome) is documented in §4 — sourced from public app screenshots and Baemin's published font families.

## Philosophy sources (G6)

### Founder/founding facts
- **우아한형제들 (Woowa Brothers) founded 2010** by **Kim Bong-jin (김봉진)** in Seoul
  - Source: [Baedal Minjok — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Baedal_Minjok), [김봉진 — 나무위키](https://namu.wiki/w/%EA%B9%80%EB%B4%89%EC%A7%84(%EA%B8%B0%EC%97%85%EC%9D%B8))
- **Founder background:** Kim Bong-jin came from a **design background** — former web designer at **NHN (now Naver)**. Unconventional founder for a tech company.
  - Source: 나무위키 김봉진
- **Origin idea:** initially a directory app for the smartphone market → pivoted to delivery (the most common phone-ordering service in Korea)
- **Unicorn within 10 years** (founded 2010 → unicorn ~2018)
- **Delivery Hero acquisition December 2019:** Delivery Hero acquired **87% of Woowa Brothers** at **$4B valuation (~₩4.75T)**
  - Investors included Hillhouse Capital, Altos Ventures, Goldman Sachs, Sequoia Capital China, Singapore's Temasek
  - Source: [Momentum Works](https://thelowdown.momentum.asia/why-might-delivery-hero-sell-its-best-asset/), [Financial News](https://www.fnnews.com/news/201912160652398539)
- **Kim Bong-jin** + management retained 13% (converted to Delivery Hero shares)
- 2023+ Kim Bong-jin pivoted to new venture **Grande Clip (그란데클립)**
  - Source: [시사저널e](https://www.sisajournal-e.com/news/articleView.html?idxno=404726)
- **Famous design principle:** *"사소한 것을 위대하게"* (Make the small things great) — Baemin design team's published philosophy for whimsical-yet-disciplined design
  - Source: [ZDNet Korea — 김봉진 새 도전](https://zdnet.co.kr/view/?no=20230920091420)

### Hannache (한나체) and the typography legacy
Baemin has released **10+ open-source Korean typefaces** (Hannache 한나체, Joojooche 주아체, Doheche 도현체, Yeonseung 연성체, Eulji-ro 을지로체, etc.) — preserving disappearing Korean signage culture from 1960s-1980s acrylic-cut letters and weathered district signs. These are a deliberate **brand differentiator** — no other Korean tech company invests in font heritage at this scale.
