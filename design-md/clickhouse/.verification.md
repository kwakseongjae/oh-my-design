## Philosophy sources (G6)

### Founder/founding facts
- **OSS Origin: ClickHouse started in 2009** as an experimental project at **Yandex** (Russia's largest tech company) — analytical reports in real-time on non-aggregated data
  - Source: [Wikipedia — ClickHouse](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ClickHouse), [ClickHouse Blog — Introducing ClickHouse Inc.](https://clickhouse.com/blog/introducing-click-house-inc)
- **First production deployment: 2012** (after 3 years of development)
- **Open-sourced 2016** under Apache 2.0 — pivotal moment; adoption by Uber, Cloudflare, eBay
- **ClickHouse Inc. incorporated September 2021** by **Aaron Katz (CEO)** + **Yury Izrailevsky** (co-founder)
  - Source: [BusinessWire — ClickHouse Inc. Series A](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20210920005219/en/ClickHouse-Inc.-Announces-Incorporation-Along-With-%2450M-In-Series-A-Funding)
- **Funding rounds:**
  - **Series A September 2021: ~$50M** led by Index Ventures + Benchmark, with Yandex N.V. participating
  - **Series B October 28, 2021: $250M at $2B valuation** led by Coatue Management + Altimeter Capital
  - **Series C May 2025: $350M at $6.35B valuation** led by Khosla Ventures, with BOND, IVP, Battery Ventures, Bessemer
  - **Total raised >$650M**
  - Source: [Wikipedia — ClickHouse](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ClickHouse), [Forkable — $6.4B valuation](https://www.forkable.io/p/open-source-database-company-clickhouse), [Index Ventures](https://www.indexventures.com/perspectives/the-fast-and-the-furious-how-clickhouse-the-worlds-fastest-open-source-database-is-creating-the-first-real-time-data-warehouse/)
