## Philosophy sources (G6)

### Founder/founding facts
- **Naver Comm founded June 2, 1999** by **이해진 (Lee Hae-jin)** in Seoul; ex-Samsung SDS.
  - Source: [Wikipedia — Lee Hae-jin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lee_Hae-jin), [Wikipedia — Naver Corporation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Naver_Corporation)
- **Founder education:** BS Computer Science, Seoul National University; MS, KAIST.
- **Pre-history:** "Web Glider" Korean search engine project began **October 1997** within Samsung SDS; first service launched January 1998; spun out 1999 with Samsung SDS seed funding.
- **Hangame merger July 2000** → renamed **NHN Corporation (Next Human Network) 2001**. KOSDAQ listing 2002, KOSPI graduation later.
- **NHN split August 1, 2013** → Naver Corporation (search/portal) + NHN Entertainment (gaming). NHN Japan parallel-split into **LINE Corporation** (web/messaging) + Hangame Japan.
- **Naver Webtoon global launch July 2014** as Line Webtoon.
- **Wattpad acquisition January 2021, US$600M** for global IP pipeline.
- **HyperCLOVA X unveiled August 24, 2023** — 204B-parameter Korean-first LLM, HCX-L / HCX-S two sizes, positioned as **sovereign AI** for Korean and Asian markets.
  - Source: [KED Global](https://www.kedglobal.com/artificial-intelligence/newsView/ked202308240018), [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2023/08/24/koreas-internet-giant-naver-unveils-generative-ai-services/), [The Register](https://www.theregister.com/2024/04/08/naver_cloud_hyperclova_llm_sovereign_ai/)
- **AI investment:** >₩1 trillion (~$3.8B USD) over 5 years; hyperscale data center designed for 600,000+ servers — Asia's largest.
- **Founder Lee Hae-jin returned as Board Chairman March 2025**; CEO Choi Soo-yeon began second term.
  - Source: [Pestel-analysis — Brief History of Naver](https://pestel-analysis.com/blogs/brief-history/navercorp), [MatrixBCG](https://matrixbcg.com/blogs/brief-history/naver)

### Open-source typography legacy
Naver has released a notable family of Korean open-source typefaces under SIL OFL:
- **Naver Maru Buri (마루부리)** — editorial myeongjo/serif
- **D2 Coding** — monospace for programming, 2016, based on Nanum BarunGothic ([github.com/naver/d2codingfont](https://github.com/naver/d2codingfont))
- **Nanum family** — Nanum Gothic, Nanum Myeongjo, Nanum Square, Nanum Pen — Korea's most widely used free web fonts

This open-source typography release functions as a deliberate brand differentiator and a public good — Korean web typography ecosystem leans on Naver's font releases the way the global web leans on Google Fonts.
