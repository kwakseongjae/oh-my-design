## Logo decision

- Google favicon proxy `https://www.google.com/s2/favicons?domain=beusable.net&sz=128` = 413B (16×16 PNG) → below the 450B floor, generic globe. Rejected.
- Brand CDN favicon `https://dream-cdn.beusable.net/home/images/favicon.ico` = HTTP 200, `image/x-icon`, 1150B, genuine brand icon. **Selected** (`type: favicon`, slug = full image URL).
- `apple-touch-icon.png` / `logo.png` on the CDN returned 403; `beusable.net/apple-touch-icon.png` 404 — CDN favicon.ico is the reliable brand asset.
