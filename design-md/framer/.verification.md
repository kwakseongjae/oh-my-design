### Single-system: 100px Pill (35px hero / 29px compact / 28px header)

Framer uses **one consistent pill geometry** across home + pricing:
- All radii **100px** (full-pill) except header utility at **15px** (rounded-rect)
- Three height tiers: 35px (hero/main) / 29px (pricing compact) / 28px (header utility)
- Three fills: `#fff` Primary, `rgba(255,255,255,0.1-0.15)` Translucent on dark hero, `#0099ff` Framer Blue (featured-tier accent)
- Font 12px·400 — never bold; the radius and surface contrast carry the weight

This confirms the §11 "design-tool-first / craft" register — pill chrome, generous whitespace, blue accent only at the moment of selection.
