### Single-system: 4px Coffee Charcoal + Warm Soft-White inverse

OpenCode uses a **warm-charcoal coffee chrome**:
- Primary: `#201d1d` Coffee Charcoal (warmer than pure `#000` — has red-cast 32R/29G/29B)
- Inverse: `#fdfcfc` Soft White (warmer than pure `#fff` — slight cream cast 253/252/252)
- All Primary CTAs: 4px radius / 40-42px / 16px·**500**
- Asymmetric padding (8×16×8×10) for icon-bearing CTAs — left-padding tighter than right
- Install-snippet tabs: 0px text-only, color-state distinguishes active (`#201d1d`) vs inactive (`#9a9898`)
- Doc sidebar active state: Light Cream `#f8f7f7` / 14px·600 (slightly weighted up from page-body 400)

The **warm-cast color discipline** (no pure black/white anywhere) reflects the SST team's coffee-shop-aesthetic Brand sensibility — distinct from pure-black tech tooling sites.

3. **Asymmetric padding** convention for icon CTAs
