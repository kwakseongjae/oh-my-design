### Official Brand Guide (WantedlyBrandGuide_v2_0.pdf, Jun 2021)
- Brand Colors: **blue400 `#21BDDB`** (RGB 33-189-219, Pantone 311U, DIC 99) · **gray800 `#292929`** (41-41-41) · **gray50 `#F5F5F5`** (245-245-245) · **white `#FFFFFF`** — with a stated Color Ratio where blue is the smallest slice and gray50/white dominate
- Typography: Latin headline **ITC Avant Garde Gothic**, Latin body **Lato**; Japanese **たづがね角ゴシック (Tazugane Gothic)**; documented fallback order: ITC Avant Garde Gothic → **Poppins**; たづがね → ヒラギノ角ゴシック (Hiragino Kaku Gothic) → Noto Sans CJK ("Poppins / Lato / Noto Sans CJK are available from Google Fonts")
- Logo: W mark (black strokes + blue400 dot) + logotype; misuse list prohibits trimming, separating mark from logotype, opacity changes, recoloring, drop shadows, outline-stroke rendering, deformation, inline-in-text use, and color logo on non-corporate-color surfaces
