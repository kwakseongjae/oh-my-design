## Philosophy sources (§10–15)

### Founder / founding facts
- **Bom Kim (김범석)** — born 1978-10-07 in Seoul; moved to US at age 7; Harvard College graduate; **dropped out of Harvard Business School in his second semester** to found Coupang in 2010.
  - Source: [Bom Kim — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bom_Kim)
  - Source: [CNBC — Coupang: Harvard dropout Bom Kim built Korea's most valuable start-up (2019-12)](https://www.cnbc.com/2019/12/04/coupang-harvard-dropout-bom-kim-built-koreas-most-valuable-start-up.html)
  - Source: [Fortune — How the 'Amazon of Asia' landed on the Fortune 500 (2023-06)](https://fortune.com/2023/06/05/coupang-fortune-500-list-stock-amazon-harvard-bom-kim/)
- **Coupang founded 2010** in Seoul as a Groupon-style daily-deals site; pivoted to end-to-end e-commerce + logistics 2013–2014.

### Rocket Delivery (`로켓배송`)
- **Launched March 2014** — same-day / next-day delivery via in-house Coupang couriers ("로켓맨" / Rocketmen).
  - Source: [Korea Times — Coupang's Rocket Delivery gaining popularity (2019-01)](https://www.koreatimes.co.kr/business/tech-science/20190129/coupangs-rocket-delivery-gaining-popularity)
  - Source: [Speedwell Research — Coupang Business History](https://www.speedwellmemos.com/p/coupang-business-history)
- **Dawn Delivery** — order by midnight, arrives by 7am, included in Wow.

### Funding milestones
- **$100M Sequoia Capital** — May 2014.
- **$300M BlackRock** — late 2014.
- **$1B SoftBank** — June 2015.
- **$2B SoftBank Vision Fund** — November 2018, valuation ~$9B.
  - Sources above + [Wikipedia — Bom Kim](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bom_Kim)

### NYSE IPO
- **Listed 2021-03-11**, NYSE ticker **CPNG**.
- First-day close $49.25; **day-one market cap ~$84.47 billion**.
- **Largest US IPO by a foreign company since Alibaba 2014**; largest US listing ever by a Korean company. **$4.6B raised.**
  - Source: [CNBC — Coupang IPO: CPNG begins trading on the NYSE (2021-03-11)](https://www.cnbc.com/2021/03/11/coupang-ipo-cpng-begins-trading-on-the-nyse.html)
  - Source: [Kim & Chang — Coupang Inc.'s IPO Listed on the NYSE](https://www.kimchang.com/en/insights/detail.kc?sch_section=2&idx=23179)
  - Source: [CNN Business — Coupang raises $4.6B in US IPO](https://www.cnn.com/2021/03/11/investing/coupang-ipo-us-stock-korea-intl-hnk)

### Sub-brands
- **Coupang Eats (`쿠팡이츠`)** — launched May 2019; "치타 배달" 30-min food delivery.
- **Coupang Play (`쿠팡플레이`)** — launched December 2020; SVOD bundled with Wow membership; SNL Korea, Premier League, Paramount+ Korea exclusive.
  - Source: [Coupang Play — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coupang_Play)
  - Source: [Korea Herald — Coupang Play exclusive Paramount+ service](https://www.koreaherald.com/article/10621055)
- **Rocket Fresh** (groceries), **Rocket Direct** (cross-border imports), **Rocket Install** (large appliances).

### Wow / 로켓와우 membership
- **7,890 KRW/month** (raised from 4,990 KRW); includes free Rocket Delivery, free returns, Coupang Eats free delivery, Coupang Play streaming.
- **Adopted by ~two-thirds of Korean households** per Korea Times reporting.
  - Source: [Korea Times — Coupang to apply increased membership fees (2024-07)](https://www.koreatimes.co.kr/business/companies/20240708/coupang-to-implement-increased-membership-fees-for-all-paid-users-next-month)
  - Source: [PYMNTS — Coupang's Loyalty Program Drives Engagement (2024)](https://www.pymnts.com/news/ecommerce/2024/coupangs-loyalty-program-drives-engagement-as-company-expands-services/)
