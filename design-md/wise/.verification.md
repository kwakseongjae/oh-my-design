Wise Design’s typography guidance independently says Inter is for most content and Wise Sans is a short, expressive display face; it permits Wise Sans for product success, progress, and feature-intro moments. The upstream Inter project publishes SIL OFL 1.1. No public Wise Sans redistribution licence was located, so neither its live loading nor its official use guidance is treated as a licence grant.
