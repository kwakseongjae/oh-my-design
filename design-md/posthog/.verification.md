### Single-system: Sage-Black + Amber-Gold + Cool Gray three-color

PostHog uses a **three-color warm-cast chrome**:
- **Primary**: `#cd8407` Amber-Gold 6-8px / 32-46px / 15-16px·400 (Sage-Charcoal text — warm-on-warm)
- **Secondary Amber**: `#eb9d2a` Light Amber 6px / 36px (slightly lighter Amber for adjacent CTAs)
- **Body text**: `#23251d` PostHog Sage-Black (warm green-cast near-black, NOT pure `#000`)
- **Cool Gray text**: `#374151` for tier-card body (a second neutral palette for content)
- **Region tint**: `rgba(247, 165, 1, 0.25)` Amber-tinted badge for data-residency
- All weight 400 except region badges (700) and nav (500)

The **Amber-Gold + Sage-Black combination** is unmistakably PostHog — a warm, almost-craft register that distinguishes from the cold-blue norm of analytics SaaS. Confirms the §11 "scrappy, honest" register.

This corrects the prior footer's "nav `#23251d` 4px ghost / 13px·500" — accurate but missed the **Amber-Gold `#cd8407` Primary** entirely (the brand-defining color).
