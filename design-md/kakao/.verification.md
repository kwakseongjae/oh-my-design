### Rendered official Kakao Login guide

The current official page states:

- Container: `#FEE500`
- Symbol: `#000000`
- Label: `#000000` at 85% opacity
- Colors outside the regulation must not be applied.
- Label font: OS default system typeface at 30Pt (Sp/Dp).
- Container radius: 12 pixels.
- The speech-bubble symbol's shape, proportion, and color cannot change; Kakao CI and functional icons cannot replace it; the button cannot omit it.
- Full labels: `카카오 로그인` / `Login with Kakao`; short labels: `로그인` / `Login`.
- When widening, preserve symbol and label zones and extend both sides equally. When scaling, preserve proportions and keep label height within one third of the container. Use the short label instead of distorting a smaller button.
