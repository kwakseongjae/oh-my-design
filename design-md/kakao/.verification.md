### Critical: dual-yellow split (intentional)

Kakao operates **TWO distinct yellows** — this is the verified critical insight:
- **`#FAE100`** = **Marketing Yellow** — corporate brand, "카카오톡 다운로드" CTA on kakaocorp.com, Kanana, freely styleable
- **`#FEE500`** = **Kakao Login Compliance Yellow** — locked by Kakao Developers SSO branding policy; mandatory for any Kakao Login implementation

Apps and brands implementing Kakao Login MUST use `#FEE500`. Marketing CTAs use `#FAE100`. Confusing them = compliance violation OR off-brand marketing.
