### Single-system: 0px sharp + Black/White + Orange-Red accent

Replicate uses **all-0px-sharp chrome** with a distinctive Orange-Red accent:
- All Primary CTAs: **0px radius** (no rounding anywhere — engineering register)
- Two fills: `#000` Black + `#fff` White (canvas-aware)
- 3 height tiers: hero 54px / page 42px / mini-secondary 34px
- All weight 400 — never bold
- **Replicate Orange-Red `#dd4425`** is the brand accent — only on featured cards (limited use)
- **Replicate Cream `#fffcfc`** featured-card surface — barely-tinted off-white
- Mid-Gray `#646464` for secondary text + inactive states
- Charcoal `#202020` for active code-tab states (warm-cast near-black)

The 0px sharpness mirrors the Cog/Docker/Rust engineering aesthetic. Confirms the §11 "OSS researcher culture + cloud convenience" register.
