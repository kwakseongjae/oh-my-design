## Philosophy sources (G6)

### Founder/founding facts
- **Spotify AB incorporated April 23, 2006** in Stockholm, Sweden
  - Source: [Daniel Ek — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daniel_Ek), [Spotify — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spotify)
- Founders: **Daniel Ek (CEO)** + **Martin Lorentzon**
- **Daniel Ek background**: former CTO of **Stardoll**; later sold ad company **Advertigo** to Lorentzon's **Tradedoubler** — that acquisition is how the two founders met
  - Source: [Soundiiz — Spotify Founders](https://soundiiz.com/blog/who-are-daniel-ek-and-martin-lorentzon-spotifys-founders/)
- **Origin idea**: Ek formed the thesis around 2002 when **Napster** was shut down and **Kazaa** took over piracy — concluded the only fix to piracy was a service better than piracy that compensates the music industry
  - Source: [Britannica — Daniel Ek](https://www.britannica.com/money/Daniel-Ek)
- **Public launch: October 7, 2008** in Sweden + select EU; **US launch July 2011**
  - Source: Wikipedia: Spotify
- **IPO direct listing on NYSE April 3, 2018** (NYSE: SPOT)
  - Source: Wikipedia: Spotify
- **Encore Design System**: Spotify's internal DS (not publicly released as Polaris/Carbon class). Documented in talks at Spotify.design + various design conferences.
