### Surface split (intentional, not conflict)

Airtable runs **two parallel button systems** for marketing-home vs product-platform:
- **Home (`airtable.com/`)** — Deep Navy `#181d26` + 12px + 16×24 + 56px + 16px·500 (generous, marketing register)
- **Platform (`airtable.com/platform`)** — Airtable Blue `#1b61c9` + 10px + 8×14 + 40px + 18px·500 (compact, product register)

The home uses Deep Navy because it's primarily showcasing AI workflows + customer logos on a clean white canvas — the navy reads as "enterprise-ready tool". The platform page uses brand blue because it dives into product/feature surfaces where the brand-color signal helps users recognize Airtable-specific UI.
