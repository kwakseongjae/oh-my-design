### Single-system: 9999px Pill, 4-tier height, weight discipline

Ollama uses a **disciplined all-pill chrome** with tiered weight + height:
- All CTAs: **9999px full-pill**
- 4 height tiers: **40px header utility** / **52px hero Create** / **38px Pro pricing** / **36px Max pricing**
- 2 weights: **400 default** / **500 hero + pricing**
- 2 fills: `#262626` Ollama Charcoal (Primary) + `#ffffff` White (inverse on dark hero)
- Padding scales: 6×16 (header) / 12×32 (hero) / 8×24 (pricing)
- Top nav is 0px text-only ghost (text-link convention, not pill chrome)

The 4-tier height system is unusually granular for a small startup brand — reflects the OSS-developer-tooling sensibility (every CTA earns its size).

This corrects the prior footer's claim — actually accurate already, but adds:
1. Pricing inverted-pill `#fff` 36-38px / 14px·500 was undocumented
2. **Hero `Create account` weight 500** distinct from header `Download` weight 400
