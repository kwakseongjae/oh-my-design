- **Pin Sans — consumer UI:** loaded/high confidence, 118 visible uses across body, buttons, headings, inputs, text, and toggles. The collector matches computed use to loaded FontFace entries and eight Pinterest-hosted sources: `Pin-Sans-Regular.ttf`, `Pin-Sans-Regular-Italic.ttf`, `Pin-Sans-Medium.ttf`, `Pin-Sans-Medium-Italic.ttf`, `Pin-Sans-Heavy.ttf`, `Pin-Sans-Heavy-Italic.ttf`, `Pin-Sans-Bold.ttf`, and `Pin-Sans-Bold-Italic.ttf` under `https://s.pinimg.com/font/`.
- **PinterestSansPro — business marketing:** loaded/high confidence, 199 visible uses across body, buttons, headings, list items, tabs, and text. The collector matches computed use to loaded FontFace entries and Pinterest-hosted regular, italic, bold, and bold-italic `Pinterest-Sans-Pro-*.otf` sources under `https://s.pinimg.com/font/`.
- **Resolution:** the two loaded families pass the required computed-use + FontFaceSet/source corroboration test, but are preserved as consumer and business-marketing families respectively. They are not collapsed into one product-wide font claim.

### Declared-only, third-party, and licence boundary

- **Declared-only:** HaasGrotDisp, HaasGrotText, PinterestSans, PinterestSansLC, PinterestSansTPJ, and PinterestUI have `@font-face` source URLs but zero visible captured usage. They remain declared-only and are not rendered as fallback specimens or promoted to tokens.
- **Third-party/unresolved:** one embedded Google Sign-In control uses `Google Sans` once, with no matching loaded FontFace or Pinterest source. It is excluded from Pinterest typography.
- **Official asset and licence boundary:** the direct `s.pinimg.com` font files are official hosted assets, but first-party web search found no public Pinterest font licence granting downstream reuse. The reference records observed family metadata and availability only; it does not treat those assets as reusable.
