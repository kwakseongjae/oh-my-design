## Philosophy sources (G6)

### Founder/founding facts
- **CTR (Computing-Tabulating-Recording Company) founded June 16 1911** by **Charles Ranlett Flint**
  - Source: [Charles Ranlett Flint — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles_Ranlett_Flint)
  - Source: [IBM — The origins of IBM](https://www.ibm.com/history/ctr-and-ibm)
- **Amalgamated four companies**: Tabulating Machine Company + International Time Recording Company + Computing Scale Company of America + Bundy Manufacturing Company
- **Renamed "International Business Machines" 1924** under Thomas J. Watson Sr. (joined CTR 1914)
- **Paul Rand hired 1956** by **Thomas J. Watson Jr.** — replaced Beton Bold with **City Medium**, created the iconic 8-bar (later 13-bar) striped IBM logo
  - Source: [History of IBM logo — Hatchwise](https://www.hatchwise.com/resources/the-history-of-the-ibm-logo)
- **Eddy Opara** — current Pentagram partner continuing the Rand legacy in IBM brand
- **Carbon Design System open-sourced 2018** ([carbondesignsystem.com](https://carbondesignsystem.com/))
- **IBM Plex font family** — IBM's open-source corporate typeface (released 2017)
- **HashiCorp acquisition** — **announced April 24 2024 for $6.4B**, **closed February 27 2025**
