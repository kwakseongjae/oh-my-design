### Achromatic CI note
The full-DOM color-frequency scan returns an **entirely grayscale palette** for chrome and structure — ink `#212126`, near-blacks `#36363d` / `#57575b` / `#62626a`, light greys `#e9e9e9` / `#dbdbdb` / `#f8f8f8`, pure white, pure black accent. The only saturated values are tiny one-off content thumbnails (e.g. `#916e44`, `#c6781d`, `#de5771`, an `#ff2222` notice red) that ride in editorial card imagery, not in the design chrome. This matches NHN's 2024 Kenya Hara rebrand, which moved off "fixed IT-company color" toward a monochromatic identity that adapts across online/offline surfaces.
