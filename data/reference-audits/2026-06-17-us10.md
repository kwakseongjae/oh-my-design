## How the 10 were researched

Same per-brand subagent pipeline as batch 1. Crucially, for US these brands largely **publish their own design systems / brand portals**, so the second (and third/fourth/fifth) surface was usually the official DS itself, live-inspected directly rather than reconstructed.

## Systemic finding

**The inverse of the KR gap.** Where KR brands were 0/10 in any public directory and forced full live reconstruction, **6/10 US brands ship a first-party, directly-inspectable design system or brand portal**: Twilio Paste (`paste.twilio.design`), Workday Canvas (`canvas.workday.com` + `@workday/canvas-colors-web` npm), Cash App (`design.cash.app`), GitLab Pajamas (`design.gitlab.com`, 5 surfaces inspected), Datadog brand/press kit, Twitch `brand.twitch.tv`. Tier-2 aggregators (getdesign/refero) remain mostly empty (only Mailchimp had a refero entry) — **and it does not matter**, because the official DS surface is the authoritative source. US strategy = capture the published system directly; treat marketing chrome as the secondary "brand" layer (most of these run a documented two-system split: brand/marketing vs product/Canvas-Paste-Pajamas).
