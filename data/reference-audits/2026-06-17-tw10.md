## Systemic finding

1. **TW mirrors the KR directory gap** — getdesign.md + refero returned **0/10**. Western catalogs under-cover Taiwan; all tokens rest on Tier-1 live inspect.
2. **Traditional-Chinese, frequently with system-CJK fallback.** Several brands (MOZE, Vocus) ship no embedded CJK webfont and fall back to the system sans for Traditional Chinese — captured honestly rather than asserting a brand webfont.
3. **i18n/geo redirects bite headless capture.** Portaly geo-redirects to `/en` globally and its `/zh-TW` path 404s to the bot, so display copy came out English (flagged). A recurring TW/marketing-site hazard.
4. **The pipeline favored live truth over my hints — twice.** Vocus's hint said "sophisticated dark theme"; the live site is actually a warm-grey/white editorial system (coral `#ff485a`, ink `#141413`, teal `#00b3c6` links) — the agent grounded in measured values and corrected the hint. Readmoo's hinted `entertainment` category is not in the canonical enum and was remapped to `consumer-tech`. Both are healthy honesty signals.
