## How the 10 were researched

Same per-brand subagent pipeline. JP is DS-rich, so the second/third/fourth/fifth surface was usually the official design system, live-inspected directly.

## Systemic finding

**JP mirrors the US "DS-first" profile, not the KR/TW gap.** Multiple brands ship genuine, inspectable public design systems: **Spindle** exposes its full token set as `:root` CSS custom properties; **DADS** is a CC BY 4.0 national government DS; **DMM Turtle** ships dark-first tokens; **GMO Pepabo Inhouse** ships brand-swappable "Flavors" tokens; **Cybozu** runs the open kintone Design System. Strategy = capture the published system directly. Two caveats recurred:

1. **DS doc pages frequently don't expose live component DOM** — they render examples as static/iframed/SPA-shadow-DOM or are literally labelled "(準備中)"/under-preparation (Spindle component pages, DADS overview docs, DMM Turtle Storybook+npm, kintone Storybook). Component geometry was therefore grounded from the production *product* surface + the DS `:root` tokens, not the doc page's example.
2. **Tier-2 directories cover JP no better than KR/TW** — getdesign.md + refero returned **0/10**. Irrelevant here because the first-party DS is authoritative.

A hint-host correction worth recording: `inhouse.pepabo.com` is NXDOMAIN; the real Inhouse DS lives at **`design.pepabo.com/inhouse`** — the agent discovered and inspected it (5 surfaces).

## Known shared limitation

The dominant JP limitation is **DS doc pages that hide their live component DOM** (static/iframe/SPA-shadow/"準備中"), forcing component geometry to come from the production product + `:root` tokens rather than the canonical doc example. Pepabo Inhouse additionally ships a deliberately neutral base flavor (brand Flavors overwrite it), so the live fills are the achromatic "pepper" base, not a single brand identity (documented inline, not a measurement gap).
